Things I love about Wattpad

Why you should read watpad!
by Nicole San Pedro

Hi! If you are one of those who love to read stories in phone, welcome to my blog! I am making this blog to express why I love Wattpad and also to let you know how much fun and enjoyable reading and using wattpad. 

Wattpad is an online storytelling community where the authors post their works such as stories, fan fiction, poems and articles too either through the website or mobile application.

This is the Home page of Wattpad you can see the unlimited stories in the app and the log in button. You can sign up for free and make your account so that you can read any stories you want and you can also make your own stories.

Prince Series by Ventrecanard

Stories

The stories on wattpad are not just pure love stories, like romantic love but also a love about family. I enjoy reading wattpad stories, whenever I am bored or have so much time. These stories that you can see in the picture is one of my favorite series, they're not just love story but you will also see the importance of life and family's love. There are also different genres like Sci-Fi, Horror, Mystery, Fiction and more.

Fandom, Friendship and more!

Interactions

No other platform online allows us to really connect with readers and fellow writers like Wattpad does. Writers in Wattpad are very kind and cheer you on strengthen your writing skills. As a readers, we cheer their on and promote their works, buying their books and support them. It's also a social site as well, they have an amazing group of friends all from different walks of life and all. We treat each other as sisters, having friendships on Wattpad community is because they share the same interest and favorite story also Author.

Famous Authors on Wattpad

Authors

There are many great authors on wattpad, they are good at using words and how their story flows. You can also see they first studied the things they wanted to put in the story before they released it. Such as the routines of Doctors Laws in our country and many others. They have a different way of writing but they are good in their own. My favorite Authors are Jonaxx, Beeyotch, and Ventrecanard. They are on a different path but I can say that they are good writers. They are my most favorite author in the wattpad world

Unlimited good stories that you will love!

Confidence

Being able to share your talent in writing with strangers and have them like your work is really amazing and definitely boost your confidence! Also having people critique your work will surely good for upgrading your talent. In Wattpad you're not just reading anyone's story, you can also make your story and share your talent in writing.
